Welcome to Tic Tac Toe!

How to Play:


Tic Tac Toe is a classic game played on a 3x3 grid.

The objective is simple: be the first to get three of your symbols in a row, column, or diagonal.

You'll play against the computer, taking turns to place your symbol ("X") on an empty cell. The computer responds with its symbol ("O"). Alternate turns until one player achieves three in a row or the grid fills up, resulting in a draw.

Choose your difficulty level, click "Let's Play!" to begin, and enjoy the challenge of this timeless game!
